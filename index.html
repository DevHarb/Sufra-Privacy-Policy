Welcome to Sufra. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly or that is generated automatically during your use of the app:

  • Personal Data: Email addresses and profiles if you create an account within the app.
  • App Content: Custom recipes, meal planning schedules, and "favorites" lists are stored securely via Firebase.
  • Usage Data: We track recipe views, search queries, and interaction with various cuisines to personalize your experience.
  • Local Settings: Language preferences (English/Arabic) and theme settings are stored locally on your device via Shared Preferences.

2. Third-Party Services

To provide a high-quality experience, we integrate the following third-party processors:

  • Google Firebase & Firestore: Used for real-time database storage, meal plan synchronization, and app maintenance mode control.
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): Used to deliver advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (like IDFA or Android Ad ID) to show interest-based ads.
  • Deep Linking: We use App Links to allow you to share recipe links with other users seamlessly.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Sync your meal plans across multiple devices.
  • Provide localized content based on your language selection.
  • Ensure the app is running the latest recipe data from our servers.
  • Maintain app stability and monitor performance.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the services. You can delete your data by contacting us or using the in-app account deletion options (where available).

5. Children's Privacy

Sufra does not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 13 years of age. If we learn that personal information from users less than 13 years of age has been collected, we will deactivate the account and take reasonable measures to promptly delete such data.
